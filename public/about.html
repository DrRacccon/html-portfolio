About Me

I have worked as a data engineer and data analyst for three years, gaining experience at both Walmart and Mercedes-Benz. During this time, I have been involved in handling large-scale data processing, building data pipelines, and ensuring data quality to support business decision-making. My role required working with various data technologies to extract, transform, and load (ETL) data efficiently, as well as optimizing databases for performance and scalability.

At Walmart, I contributed to managing and analyzing retail data, helping to drive insights for inventory and customer behavior. At Mercedes-Benz, I worked with automotive data, supporting analytics for vehicle performance and production efficiency. My experience across these industries has given me a strong foundation in data management and analytics, allowing me to work with diverse datasets and contribute to data-driven solutions.
