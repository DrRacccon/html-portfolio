Best Movies of all Time

My personal opinion on the best movies ever

Stalker (1979)

Russian sci fi movie about a group of travelers going into a zone that can grant wishes.

Blader Runner (1982)

Special hunters called "Blade Runners" are tasked to hunt down runaway androids.

Melancholia (2011)

A wandering planet is about to collide with Earth, while a group of people has to deal with it.

Summary

1. Stalker
2. Blade Runner
3. Melancholia
